Minimax Algorithm for Learning Rotations

Wojciech Kotłowski, Manfred K. Warmuth ; JMLR W&CP 19:821-824, 2011.

Abstract

It is unknown what is the most suitable regularization for rotation matrices and how to maintain uncertainty overrotations in an online setting.We propose to address these questions by studying the minimax algorithm for rotations and begin by working out the 2-dimensional case.
